About to Say

Crossbill International is a leading exporter of premium handicraft products based in Tanda, near Moradabad, a city globally recognized for its handicraft products. We are committed to bringing the rich heritage of Indian craftsmanship to a global market. We endeavor to place our brand on the global map in the field of handicrafts and contribute towards the growth of this industry.

We offer an extensive range of wooden and iron furniture, wall and home décor items, and various decorative showpieces with multiple finishes to suit every customer's preference. Our designs are contemporary and minimalistic, perfectly suited for the modern world. We take your concepts and bring them to life with our product design and engineering teams that work together to cater to your ideas.

We have collaborated with dedicated and supremely talented artisans and craftsmen, from across the country so that we can deliver the highest quality products to our customers. Our motto is to create a perfect blend of the contemporary and classical and increase the reach of our rich handicrafts industry to all corners of the world.


Our Vision

Our vision at Crossbill International is to be a global leader in the handicraft industry, renowned for our exceptional quality and innovative designs. We aspire to bridge the gap between traditional craftsmanship and modern aesthetics, creating timeless products that resonate with customers worldwide. By fostering sustainable practices and empowering our artisans, we aim to inspire appreciation for the rich cultural heritage of Indian handicrafts and contribute to the industry's vibrant future.

Our Mission

At Crossbill International, our mission is to preserve and promote the rich heritage of Indian craftsmanship on a global scale. We strive to create high-quality, contemporary, and minimalistic handicraft products that cater to diverse tastes and styles. By collaborating with talented artisans from across India, we aim to blend traditional techniques with modern design, ensuring authenticity and superior craftsmanship. Our goal is to expand the global reach of India’s handicraft industry, contributing to its growth while adhering to ethical business practices and ensuring fair compensation for our artisans.
